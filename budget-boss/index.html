How the game works

You begin the month with a checking account balance and a stack of real-world decisions. Each round has a fixed bill plus a scenario. Your choices affect your money and your financial decision score.

  • There are 10 rounds in the game.
  • A mid-month paycheck arrives in Round 6.
  • Your goal is to finish with a healthy balance and a strong score.
  • At the end, use the copy button to grab your submission summary.

Winning mindset

You do not have to pick the cheapest option every time. Good budgeting is about balance, planning, and avoiding flashy choices that punch holes in the wallet.

  • Protect cash for bills first.
  • Think twice before financing wants.
  • Short-term comfort can create long-term damage.
  • Steady choices usually beat dramatic ones.
