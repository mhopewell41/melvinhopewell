About the Suite

Finance concepts, minus the cardboard flavor.

The Melvin Hopewell Finance Suite brings together interactive tools and games that help students practice personal finance in a more engaging way. Each activity is designed to reinforce practical decision-making while keeping the experience approachable and useful in a classroom setting.
