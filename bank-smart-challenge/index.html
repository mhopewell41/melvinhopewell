Can you run your bank life without feeding it to fees, fraud, and bad clicks? 🏦

Play through 10 banking decisions involving checking, savings, debit cards, overdrafts, mobile deposits, alerts, and account safety. Make smart moves, protect your money, and finish with your banking habits polished like chrome.

How the game works

You start with a checking balance, a small savings account, and a banking skill score. Each round presents a realistic banking choice. Good decisions help your money and reduce risk.

  • There are 10 rounds.
  • Good choices improve your banking skill score.
  • Poor choices can raise fees or fraud risk.
  • You will manage checking, savings, alerts, and account safety.
  • At the end, copy your summary for Canvas.

Banking wisdom

Good banking is not flashy. It is a quiet machine that keeps your money from rolling downhill into a ditch.

  • Track your balance.
  • Use savings like a shield, not confetti.
  • Watch for fees.
  • Turn on alerts.
  • Treat suspicious messages like trapdoors wearing disguises.
