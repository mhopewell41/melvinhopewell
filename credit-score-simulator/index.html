Play through 10 credit decisions. Payment history, utilization, applications, and smart habits all affect your score. Finish strong and prove you can guide a credit profile without driving it into a hedge.

How the game works

You begin with a starting credit score, one open credit card, and a series of realistic choices. Each round changes your score and credit profile based on how responsibly you manage borrowing.

  • There are 10 rounds.
  • Payment history matters the most.
  • Keeping balances low helps utilization.
  • Too many new applications can nick your score.
  • At the end, copy your summary for Canvas.

Score wisdom

Good credit is usually built with steady, almost boring behavior. That is the sneaky superpower.

  • Pay on time.
  • Keep card balances low.
  • Do not open accounts just because a cashier smiles and says points.
  • Fix errors when they show up.
  • Use credit carefully, not fearfully.
