Survive tax season with your dignity, your documents, and at least some of your sanity. 🧾

Play through 10 tax-time situations involving forms, deadlines, withholding, refunds, deductions, and filing choices. Make smart decisions, avoid penalty potholes, and finish the season like a paperwork wizard.

How the game works

You start with a tax confidence score, a filing readiness score, and a few lurking risks. Each round presents a tax decision. Strong choices raise your confidence and lower your risk.

  • There are 10 rounds.
  • Good choices build tax confidence.
  • Bad choices raise audit or penalty risk.
  • You will meet W-2s, 1099s, deadlines, refunds, and deductions.
  • At the end, copy your summary for Canvas.

Tax survival tips

Taxes reward preparation. They are less a monster and more a very picky filing cabinet.

  • Keep records.
  • Know which forms match your income.
  • Do not confuse a refund with free money.
  • File on time, or request an extension if needed.
  • Claim only deductions and credits you truly qualify for.
