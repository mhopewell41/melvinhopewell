How Boss Battle works

You will face 20 multiple choice questions pulled from across the whole finance suite. Correct answers build your score and streak. Each question also gives a quick explanation, so the battle doubles as review.

  • There are 20 questions.
  • Each correct answer earns 5 points.
  • Wrong answers break your streak, but the explanation still teaches the concept.
  • Your end screen includes a submission summary you can copy into Canvas.
